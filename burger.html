How to make a burger

Ingredients

  • 1 burger bun
  • 1 beef patty
  • 2 Slices of bacon
  • Sliced avacado
  • 1 slice of cheese
  • 1 lettuce leaf
  • 2 slices of tomato
  • 1 tablespoon of ketchup
  • 1 tablespoon of mustard

    Mustard is not requireg but you can add if you prefer

  • 1 tablespoon of mayonnaise
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. Preheat the grill to medium-high heat.
  2. Season the beef patty with salt and pepper.
  3. Grill the beef patty for 4-5 minutes on each side, or until cooked to your liking.
  4. While the patty is cooking, cook the bacon in a skillet until crispy.
  5. Toast the burger bun on the grill for 1-2 minutes.
  6. Assemble the burger by placing the beef patty on the bottom bun.
  7. Add the cheese slice on top of the patty and let it melt.
  8. Add the bacon, avocado, lettuce, tomato, ketchup, mustard, and mayonnaise.
  9. Top with the other half of the bun and serve hot.
